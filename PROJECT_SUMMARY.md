# SkiFi Designs - Premium Landing Page

## 🎨 Design Overview
A conversion-optimized, premium landing page built for SkiFi Designs - a presentation design studio. The design follows Apple-inspired minimalism with a dark luxury theme and blue accent colors (#2A7AFE, #3B82F6).

## ✨ Key Features Implemented

### 1. **Hero Section**
- Stunning professional background image
- Compelling headline with gradient text
- Two prominent CTAs (Book a Free Call, View Portfolio)
- Real-time stats display (7+ years, 2,700+ slides, 100+ clients)
- Smooth scroll indicator animation

### 2. **Trusted By Section**
- Showcases real client brands (Coca-Cola, Toyota, Verizon, Indigo, etc.)
- Interactive brand cards with hover effects
- Statistics showcase with gradient accents

### 3. **Services Section**
- 6 core services displayed in cards
- Lucide-react icons (no emoji)
- Hover animations with scale and glow effects
- Clear service descriptions

### 4. **Why SkiFi Section**
- 8 key differentiators with checkmark indicators
- Clean list layout with hover effects
- Professional copywriting

### 5. **Portfolio Section**
- Real project images from Behance
- 6 featured projects with interactive cards
- External links to full portfolio
- Hover effects with image zoom and overlays

### 6. **Process Section**
- 4-step workflow visualization
- Large gradient numbers (01-04)
- Connected flow on desktop
- Clear step descriptions

### 7. **Testimonials Section**
- 3 client testimonials with 5-star ratings
- Real names, roles, and companies
- Professional card design

### 8. **Founder Section**
- Personal introduction from Ishaque
- Authentic brand story
- Clean, professional layout

### 9. **FAQ Section**
- Shadcn Accordion component
- 5 common questions answered
- Smooth animations

### 10. **Final CTA Section**
- Bold call-to-action
- Two conversion buttons
- Subtle blue glow effect

### 11. **Header & Footer**
- Sticky header with smooth navigation
- Logo integration
- Complete footer with links and contact info

## 🎯 Design Guidelines Followed
✅ Dark luxury theme with blue accents
✅ Apple-inspired minimalism
✅ No inappropriate gradients (under 20% rule)
✅ Lucide-react icons (no emoji)
✅ Shadcn UI components throughout
✅ Micro-animations on all interactions
✅ Premium spacing and typography
✅ Responsive design
✅ Real content integration
